About us

ItalAI, is a technology accelerator and startup incubator aiming to revolutionize the landscape of AI development. Founded on a rich academic foundation from esteemed institutions and with strong connections with Silicon Valley, we bring a unique blend of theoretical expertise and practical know-how to the table. At ItalAI, we advocate a modern approach to AI development, seamlessly integrating cutting-edge research methodologies with the creation of groundbreaking AI technologies.

Our mission

Our mission is to advance AI innovation by pursuing the latest advancements and applying them in real-world scenarios. We accelerate new AI technologies, incubate projects into startups, and spin them out as independent businesses. Inspired by Silicon Valley, this unique approach in Italy aims to shape the future of AI and humanity. Join us on this journey.
