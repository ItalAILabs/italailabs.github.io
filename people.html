Prof. Fabio Galasso

Founder

Prof. Fabio Galasso is the co-founder of ItalAI. At ItalAI, Fabio leads applied research in the field of computer vision and machine learning, working with the scientists and our partners to define new solutions for each novel technological challenge.

Fabio strives for defining the new state-of-the-art in the theory and application fields of ItalAI. Fabio is passionate about technology and about the technological transfer of ideas into products. In addition to his role at ItalAI, Fabio heads the Perception and Intelligence Lab (PINLab) at Sapienza University of Rome, where he conducts fundamental research in AI. Prior to this, Fabio founded and directed the Computer Vision Department at OSRAM (Munich, Germany), an international team conducting R&D in artificial intelligence, computer vision and machine learning, in relation to smart lighting applications. Fabio also conducted research on video analysis and segmentation, scene understanding and clustering at the University of Cambridge (UK) and at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics (Germany).

Fabio has served as area and industrial chair at international top conferences, as reviewer for journals and conferences, and as co-chair of international workshops.

Fabio's innovation transfer successes include the VISN product, which was awarded the 2019 IoT/WT Innovation World Cup, the 2019 Digital Champions Award, and the 2018 Deutscher Digital Award. Fabio is inventor in 30 granted patents.
