Our Research and Publications

Following the Human Thread In Social Navigation

This research explores how AI systems can better understand and predict human movement in social environments by analyzing subtle cues and interactions. The goal is to develop navigation systems that are more intuitive and respectful of human behavior.

LongCodeBench

LongCodeBench focuses on evaluating and improving the capabilities of large language models (LLMs) in handling and generating long-form code. This project addresses the challenges of maintaining coherence and correctness over extended code sequences, crucial for complex software development tasks.

Human Motion Unlearning

This research investigates methods for "unlearning" specific human motion data from AI models. This is critical for privacy, security, and ethical considerations, allowing models to remove sensitive or unwanted motion patterns while retaining general knowledge.
