Workshop on Perception and Intelligence

February 2–6, 2026 · Hotel Nido dell’Aquila · Fonte Cerreto

About the Workshop

The Workshop on Perception and Intelligence brings together researchers working on machine learning, computer vision, and human-centered AI. Participants will exchange ideas, present ongoing research, and discuss future directions in a focused and collaborative environment.

The event is hosted at Hotel Nido dell’Aquila, in the area of Fonte Cerreto, providing an ideal setting for concentrated work and structured scientific exchange.

Program Overview

The workshop is structured around a series of thematic sessions. Each session features selected presentations followed by moderated discussions. Additional working periods are included in the schedule to encourage collaboration and in-depth technical exchange.
